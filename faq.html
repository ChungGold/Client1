To see if you are qualified for semi-permanent makeup, PLEASE go over our list of important eligibility qualifications that pertain to your health before booking your appointment. Failure to do so will result in refusal of service and non-refundable deposit.


NOT recommended for those who are:


  • Under the age of 18
  • Pregnant or nursing
  • Affected by skin irritations & conditions not limited to, but including eczema, psoriasis, keloid disorder, rashes, sunburns, acne, etc. near the treated areas
  • Diabetic (consult your doctor)
  • Undergoing Chemotherapy or Radiotherapy (consult your doctor)
  • Affected by viral infections and/or diseases
  • Affected by epilepsy
  • Have a pacemaker or major heart issues
  • Had an organ transplant
  • Sick (cold, flu, etc)
  • Affected by glaucoma and /or taking blood thinning medicines
  • Had botox in the past 2 months (we recommend doing microblading first)
  • Users of Accutane, antasure and steroids in the past year
  • Under doctor's care/taking medication. Please message us first.
  • HAVE PREVIOUS SEMI-PERMENENT MAKEUP- PLEASE SEND US A CLEAR PICTURE OF YOUR BARE BROWS FOR CONSIDERATION
